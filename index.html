The Sound

tensorplex uses Logic Pro X DAW for production, mixing and mastering. Native Instruments Traktor S4 is his hardware of choice when playing live. Favourite plugin? Easily - OTT by Xfer Records!

His music style is best characterized by edgy/arpy progressive house synths, over smooth/deep Moog-like baselines, at times minimal and subsequently very hard hitting.

tensorplex

tensorplex, real name Alex, is from London, UK. He produces various styles, from chilled ambient scores to alt RnB/Hip-Hop tracks. But his real passion is in progressive house and dance music.

You can usually catch tensorplex at the next Open Frequencies session at the legendary Troubadour music venue in London.
