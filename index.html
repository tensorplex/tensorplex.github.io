_sound

tensorplex uses Logic Pro X DAW for production, mixing and mastering. Native Instruments Traktor S4 is his hardware of choice when playing live. Favourite plugin? Easily - OTT by Xfer Records!

Producing various styles, ranging from chilled ambient scores to alt RnB/Hip-Hop tracks, tensorplex's heart is always in progressive house.

His music style is best characterized by edgy/arpy progressive house synths, over smooth/deep baselines, at times minimal and subsequently very hard hitting.

Download tensorplex's royalty free samples on Freesound.org.

_bio

tensorplex is from London, UK, he grew up listening to Jungle, Drum & Bass, UK Garage, House genres - alongside Enigma (Michael Cretu), Pink Floyd and Faithless.

Having dabbled a little bit with music production previously, tensorplex started producing during the Summer of 2017 after attending Coachella Music & Arts festival in California.

With a background in computer programming, tensorplex is particularly interested in the intersection of electronic music and technology.
